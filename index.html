    Recipient of James B. Wiley Endowed PhD Fellowship
    Jim Wiley received his PhD at the UW Business School in 1975. He went on to a distinguished academic career at such institutions as Wayne State University, Temple University, University of Alberta, and Victoria University in New Zealand. Much of his research involved development and testing of psychological measurement methods such as multidimensional scaling and conjoint analysis, as well as their application to marketing problems.
